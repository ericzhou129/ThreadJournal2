Design Rationale

  • Light blue (#E8F3FF) maintains the calm, zen aesthetic
  • Subtle shadow adds just enough depth without being distracting
  • Rounded corners (10px radius) feel softer and more organic
  • Inline-flex display keeps the timestamp naturally positioned
  • Minimal padding (2px vertical, 10px horizontal) keeps it compact
  • Color remains gray (#8E8E93) to avoid overwhelming the content
  • The blue circle makes timestamps easier to scan visually

Technical Implementation

  • Replace .entry-timestamp class with .entry-timestamp-circle
  • Ensure Dynamic Type support is maintained
  • Test with various timestamp lengths (e.g., "Just now", "5 years ago")
  • Verify accessibility - ensure sufficient contrast ratio
  • Consider adding subtle animation on entry creation
